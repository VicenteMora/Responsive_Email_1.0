We need some money for our flag!
We are currently in need of a new flag. We recently got awarded with a prestigous recognition that has not been met in the state of florida for the last 40 yrs. Our flag is old, its time to retire her.
Everytime that there is a donation to our non-profit it is a step closer to our goal. A small donation goes a long way for this group of veterans who sacrificed thier lives for this country.
 
Donations to a non-profit are tax deductible and are always appreciated. There is a generation of war fighters that are on endangered and we need to provide these veterans with the same care that they provided this country.
